How is automation changing the economy

According to the Whitehouse, AI-driven automation will transform the economy over the coming years and decades. The challenge for policymakers will be to update, strengthen, and adapt policies to respond to the economic effects of AI. However, with every major change in a new economy new jobs are created. Here is table showing salary statistics for the top 5 paying jobs in the future.








Title Description Median Salary Projected Growth
Software Developer Software developers invent the technologies that we sometimes take for granted every day. $100,080 253,400 jobs
Information Security Analyst Information security analysts are the gatekeepers or security guards of information systems. $92,600 28,400 jobs
IT Manager Computer and information systems managers, or information technology managers, are the guides who help organizations navigate the always-changing labyrinth that is modern technology. $135,800 43,800 jobs
Computer Systems Analyst Computer systems analysts leverage both their knowledge of information technology and business to design better computer systems and processes for their clients. $87,220 53,000 jobs
Computer Network Architect Computer network architects design, build and maintain a variety of data communication networks, from expansive cloud infrastructures to smaller intranets. $101,210 10,400 jobs

These jobs were ranked by US News World report. For more info, visit US News.
