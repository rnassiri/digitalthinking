What is artificial intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) makes it possible for machines to learn from experience, adjust to new inputs and perform human-like tasks.

Pros

With artificial intelligence, the chances of error are almost nil and greater precision and accuracy is achieved.
Artificial intelligence finds applications in space exploration. Intelligent robots can be used to explore space.
Intelligent robots can be programmed to reach the Earth's nadirs. They can be used to dig for fuels.
Intelligent machines can replace human beings in many areas of work. Robots can do certain laborious tasks

Cons

One of the main disadvantages of artificial intelligence is the cost incurred in the maintenance and repair.
An important concern regarding the application of artificial intelligence is about ethics and moral values. Is it ethically correct to create replicas of human beings?
Machines may be able to store enormous amounts of data, but the storage, access, and retrieval is not as effective as in case of the human brain.
If robots begin to replace humans in every field, it will eventually lead to unemployment.

However, let's not forget robots like to have fun too. Meet the world's most curious robot, Wall-e!
